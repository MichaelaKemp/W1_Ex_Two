Cockatiel

The cockatiel, also known as the weero/weiro or quarrion, is a medium-sized parrot that is a member of its own branch of the cockatoo family endemic to Australia.

Cockatiels Favorite things in order!

  1. Perches
  2. Shredding Toys
  3. Natural Foliage
